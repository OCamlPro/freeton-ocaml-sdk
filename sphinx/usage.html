How to use ft¶

Creating a new Smart Contract¶

If you have a file Contract.sol, you can use:

ft contract --build Contract.sol

to build it and register in the contract database of ft. ft will perform the following calls:

$HOME/.ft/bin/solc Contract.sol
$HOME/.ft/bin/tvm_linker compile --abi-json Contract.abi.json Contract.code --lib ~/.ft/bin/stdlib_sol.tvm

So, you must make sure that solc and tvm_linker are correctly installed for ft:

  1. Follow the steps in https://github.com/tonlabs/TON-Solidity-Compiler, and copy the resulting files (solc from build/solc and stdlib_sol.tvm from lib/stdlib_sol.tvm) into $HOME/.ft/bin/
  2. Follow the steps from https://github.com/tonlabs/TVM-linker, and copy tvm_linker/target/release/tvm_linker into $HOME/.ft/bin/
